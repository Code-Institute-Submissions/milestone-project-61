About you

Our team provide Scotland's wildest guided expeditions. In the safe hands of our experienced instructors you'll find there's no peak too high, no river too wild, no trail too rugged. Scottish weather, unpredictable as it may be, is never an obstacle.

Between us we have over 18 years of military experience, have logged over 1500 quality mountain days, and hold 32 oudoor activities qualifications with British awarding bodies. Whatever the adventure you have in mind, our team have the skills and experience to make it happen.

Before moving to Scotland, Andrew grew up in the Lake District and served 9 years with the Royal Engineers. He made the move up to Scotland in 2015 and became a Search and Rescue technician with the Cairngorms Mountain Rescue Team.

Andrew is a qualified Mountain Leader, Climbing Instructor and a Swift Water Rescue Technician - Skills he puts to good use when taking groups out into the Highlands and showing them the variety of environments it has to offer.

Connaire learned his outdoor skills in the harsh terrain of the Brecon Beacons, going on to join the Royal Welsh Regiment and driving tanks in some of the worlds most hospitable environments. Connaire enjoys the relative peace and quiet of the Cairngorms.

Whether it's sailing along a serene loch, or paddling down one of the Cairngorm's many wild rivers, it's from the water that Connaire thinks you get the most out of the Highlands. With a fleet of canoes, kayaks and rafts, and even a power boat, a water journey can be included in any expedition.

The Cairngorms have always been home to Adam. After leaving home to join the Scots Dragoon Guards in Fife, he returned to found Cairngorms Outdoors in 2019, setting it up just outside his home village of Braemar. Since then, the company has brought outdoor adventures to over 300 groups!

Adam loves bringing people to the highest parts of the Cairngorms, be it the summit of Ben Macdui, Bynack More, or Cairn Gorm itself. Whatever the weather, climbing up above the clouds means stunning views and amazing memories are guaranteed.

Oppo is by far the most loved member of the team, but he isn't just a cute face. He's a fully trained Search Dog with the Cairngorms Mountain Rescue Team and reached stardom when he featured on Channel Five's "Callout: Search and Rescue".

Never letting the fame and glory get to his head, Oppo is a seriously chilled out little guy. If he's not out on an expedition or rescue mission, he can usually be found laying by the fire at HQ.
