About you

Our team provide Scotland's wildest guided expeditions. In the safe hands of our experienced instructors you'll find there's no peak too high, no river too wild, no trail too rugged. Scottish weather, unpredictable as it may be, is never an obstacle.

Between us we have over 18 years of military experience, have logged over 1500 quality mountain days, and hold 32 oudoor activities qualifications with British awarding bodies. Whatever the adventure you have in mind, our team have the skills and experience to make it happen.

Before moving to Scotland, Andrew grew up in the Lake District and served 9 years with the Royal Engineers. He made the move up to Scotland in 2015 and became a Search and Rescue technician with the Cairngorms Mountain Rescue Team.
